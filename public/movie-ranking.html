The Best Movies According to Deadpool

My top 3 movies of all time.

October

A group of interns are going through the usual grind when suddenly an accident changes their lives.
The protagonist can't get let go of it and becomes obsessed with it.
What does it all lead to? Is it love or something else?

The Batman

When a sadistic serial killer begins murdering key political figures in Gotham,
Batman is forced to investigate the city's hidden corruption and question his family's involvement.

Deadpool

A wisecracking mercenary gets experimented on and becomes immortal yet hideously scarred,
and sets out to track down the man who ruined his looks.
